How It Works

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Create an account or log in to the platform.
  2. Fill out the corruption report form with detailed information.
  3. Submit your report securely and anonymously.
  4. Our team will review and investigate the report.
  5. Follow-up actions will be taken based on the investigation findings.

Benefits

  • Anonymity: Your identity is kept confidential to protect you from retaliation.
  • Transparency: All reports are handled transparently, ensuring accountability.
  • Accountability: Your reports contribute to holding perpetrators accountable for their actions.
